About Pocket Job

Pocket Job is a modern job portal designed to connect job seekers and recruiters across Nepal in a simple and efficient way.

Our Mission

Our goal is to make job searching easy, fast, and accessible for everyone — from students looking for part-time work to professionals seeking full-time opportunities.

What We Offer

  • ✔ Easy job application system
  • ✔ Real-time recruiter communication
  • ✔ Secure job posting with payment integration
  • ✔ Personalized dashboard for applicants and recruiters

Why Pocket Job?

Unlike traditional job portals, Pocket Job focuses on simplicity, speed, and real user experience. Whether you're hiring or job hunting, everything is designed to be straightforward and effective.
